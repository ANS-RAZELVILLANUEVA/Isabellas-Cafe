Kape ta?

Same taste, bigger indulgence. Treat yourself to more layers of coffee and cream.

Isabella’s Cafe sits directly on the Marina Sea Wall in San Jose, Antique, offering the best seats in town for a Sulu Sea sunset. The open-air design catches the coastal breeze, making it a favorite local hangout where the sound of crashing waves serves as the natural soundtrack to your meal.

The vibe is effortlessly relaxed, blending rustic decor with a front-row view of the horizon. It’s the go-to spot for anyone wanting to experience Antique’s "slow life," providing a peaceful escape where you can grab a coffee and watch the sky change colors over the waterfront.
